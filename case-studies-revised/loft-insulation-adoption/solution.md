# Solution: Loft Insulation Adoption

This was tested in the real world.

The UK Department of Energy and Climate Change, working with the Behavioural
Insights Team, tested whether removing the household hassle would improve uptake.
The intervention offered loft clearance alongside loft insulation, so residents
did not have to clear stored belongings before installers could work.

The trial suggested that loft clearance could increase completed installations,
especially at the lower clearance price, but the official report says the sample
was too small to draw firm conclusions.
