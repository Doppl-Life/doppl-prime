# Solution: The Rock Star Drone Problem

This was solved in the real world.

The yacht detected incoming paparazzi drones before they reached useful filming
range. Instead of shooting them down, jamming them, or staging a visible security
response, the detection system automatically triggered a private song cue through
the yacht's sound system. Only the owner and the people he had told understood what
it meant.

When the song played, the owner and the relevant people moved inside the yacht, out of exposed sightlines, and the obvious visual paths were closed. By the time the drone arrived, it could still film the yacht, but not the photographs it came for.

The useful move was not defeating the drone as an object. It was making the drone's
footage worthless while keeping the night discreet and mostly uninterrupted.
