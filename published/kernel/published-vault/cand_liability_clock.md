# Liability Clock Transfer

Treat legal liability milestones as the earliest public clock for ownership unwind rather than waiting for unit sales to fall.

## Mechanism

Track insurer filings, state deployment permissions, and OEM liability assumptions as the moment the driver stops being the legal subject.

## Claimed Delta

Moves the forecast upstream from visible car sales decline to the first point where risk ownership shifts away from households.
