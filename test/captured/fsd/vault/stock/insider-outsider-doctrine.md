# Insider Outsider Doctrine

Domain memory admitted by the judge from discovery.

## Load-bearing facts

### Rapid structural change leaves insider/outsider doctrines misaligned with who actually holds local knowledge.

Merton treats insider and outsider status as contested doctrines that lag behind social restructuring, not as fixed credentials. An actor with the correct on-the-ground model can be epistemically inside the situation while remaining outside every party's recognition doctrine, with no legitimate path to convert knowledge into institutional influence.
