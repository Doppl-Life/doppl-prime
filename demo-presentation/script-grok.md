## 6 · The Human in the Loop

The pipeline does everything between the question and the action.

You keep two jobs. **Ask.** **Enact.**

The machine took the research. It took the branching. It took the scoring. It politely left you the consequence — skin in the game on the last arrow. Your move.

## 7 · Implications & Opportunity

We're coining a category.

**PSaaS** — Problem-Solving as a Service — also known as… **Pizzazz.**

Consulting has always been this. McKinsey, the boutiques, the strategy decks — someone gets paid to recover the problem and find the unlock. The infrastructure was just unscalable. Unreproducible. One partner's brain, one engagement at a time.

We make it scalable. Reproducible. Replayable.

Down the road: converse with the whole garden — find the through-line across every lineage. Hook your software factory to the output — landing pages spun up, niche markets tested, validation waiting in the world before you've finished the deck.

And the market? That whole collapsing problem-economy from the cold open — the lawyers, the trauma units, the ticket towns, every institution paid per incident or paid to maintain capacity for incidents that stop arriving — that's not a tragedy slide. That's the size of the opportunity.

The dominoes are the market. The opportunity is the vacuum.
