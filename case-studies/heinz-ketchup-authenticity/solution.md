# Solution: Heinz Ketchup Bottle Authenticity

This was solved in the real world.

Heinz and Wunderman Thompson Turkey matched the exact red of Heinz ketchup and
printed that color on the edge of the bottle label. If the bottle was refilled
with another ketchup, the sauce visible through the bottle would no longer match
the label.

The package became its own comparison standard. The customer did not need a
scanner, seal, staff explanation, or enforcement process; the mismatch was
visible at the table.
