# Solution: The Swim-Up Boarding

This problem has a known real-world yacht-security architecture.

The perimeter is extended into the water, not only the deck, gangway, doors, and
cameras. The reference architecture combines underwater intruder-detection sonar
with above-water camera or thermal coverage, classifies likely human movement
separately from wildlife, debris, crew activity, and small craft, then sends one
clear alert early enough for the security team to intercept before boarding.

The important part is not adding more passive CCTV. It is turning a quiet
waterline approach into a classified event that triggers a rehearsed response.
