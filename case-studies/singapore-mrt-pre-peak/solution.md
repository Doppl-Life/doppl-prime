# Solution: Singapore Rail Peak Crowding

This has been addressed in the real world.

Singapore uses fare incentives to move flexible riders out of the morning peak.
The island-wide Morning Pre-Peak Fares scheme gives up to 50 cents off weekday
rail trips when commuters tap in before 7:45am.

As of December 27, 2025, Singapore also scheduled free morning off-peak rail
rides on the North East Line and Sengkang-Punggol LRT for qualifying weekday
tap-ins before 7:30am or between 9:00am and 9:45am, alongside enhanced Travel
Smart Journeys rewards.
